FAQ's

Can I get any Income Tax benefits if I donate to your organization?
Yes, Our NGO is a government registered organization. All donations are exempted from Income tax & benefit is availed under section of 80 G.
What is the impact of my donation to your NGO?
Your generous support helps in transforming lives, Every rupee you donate, is used to create a brighter, more secure future for children & adolescents we serve. Every donation seeds a plant of hope. You are not just giving funds—you are giving childhood, safety, and a future.
How can I obtain receipt of my donation?
  • Donation receipts are sent to the donor via email or official correspondence.
  • If you have any query you can contact us at - +91 7588714342
    Or write us at - chaitnya.mauli123@gmail.com
  • Updates about the impact of your donation are only sent if you opt-in.
Can I donate in any other form instead of monetary ( money) donation?
Yes, We accept donations in other forms like donations of grains, edible oil cans (approved by FDA), sugar or jaggery ( all minimum 5 kg or more), fresh fruits, toys, sponsorships of daycare dresses, mats, notebooks, story books, dry snacks.
If I wish to visit your NGO; what are the timings ?

We warmly welcome visitors who wish to understand more about our work and interact with the children and staff.

1] Creche

Visiting Days: Monday to Saturday

Timings: 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM

Sunday & Public Holidays: Closed (except on special events)

2] Sanskar Varg

At majority of the places timing is 4 to 6pm but the timings & days differ as per the location.

Note:

To ensure a meaningful visit and minimize disruption to children's routine, we request you to kindly inform us at least 1–2 days in advance.
