How it started

It was some 40 years back, The Chaitanya Mauli Vishwast Mandal was established by an entrepreneur Shri. Vaman Shankar Joshi along with his wife, Malati Joshi and some of their like-minded friends, with vision to develop morally upright, responsible and cultured future generation, who would contribute in upliftment of their own brothers and sisters, as well as for betterment of society and Nation.

What made origin of Daycare?

When Shri. Vaman Shankar Joshi alongwith his wife Malati Joshi planned to recide in Panchsheel Nagar, they observed that, the area was housed by hard working, hand to mouth labours, facing challenging social conditions. Consequently the children growing up in this area were facing various difficulties. Some were drawn in alcohol, tobacco, gutkha addiction, gambling and other social vices. It was very painful to see the Neglected Growth and Squandering potential of such young children. They both strongly belived that moral and ethical education plays important role in ensuring the future of young children. Hence Shri Chaitanya mauli Vishwasta Mandal Trust was established to help children to grow with love and care. Holistic development of children can be achived by instilling strong ethics, fostering values at early age,not only to help children navigate life's challengs but also contribute to the develpment as a responsible citizen.
