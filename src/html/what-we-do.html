What we do

We always strive to launch impactful initiatives that help society grow, evolve, and empower every individual.

A second home for little hearts to blossom where imaginations soar and friendship grow, providing a safe,nurturing envirnment filled with warmth care laughter, learning and creativity ensuring the children thrive well while their parents focus on jobs, this has been running since 1966.

Counselling center

A safe and supportive space that promotes mental well-being, where individuals receive guidance on time management, study techniques, and stress management to overcome challenges and develop healthy coping skills. The center aims to empower individuals to build confidence and enhance their overall emotional resilience.

Immunization Center

Immunization Center and Health Care: Regular immunization services are provided, with sessions scheduled on the second Tuesday of every month. These services are conducted by the SMKC Corporation Health Centre, Jamwadi.Regular health check-ups by expert doctors are organized, and appropriate medical advice is offered to those in need.
