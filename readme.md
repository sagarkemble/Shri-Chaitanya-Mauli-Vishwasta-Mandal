# Shri Chaitanya Mauli Viswasta Mandal

</div>

Shri Chaitanya Mauli Vishwasta Mandal is a registered charitable trust established in **1982** (NGO) located in Sangli, Maharashtra, dedicated to serving the underprivileged sector of society.  
The organization provides:

- **Daycare** – Safe and nurturing space for children.
- **Sanskar Varg** – Values and ethics education for kids.
- **Immunization Center** – Health and disease prevention services.
- **Library** – Access to books and learning resources.
- **Vocational Training** – Skill development for youth.
- **Bachat Ghat** – Encouraging financial awareness and saving.
- **Counselling Center** – Mental well-being support.
- **Study Center** – Academic guidance and learning support.
